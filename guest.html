Guest Info

We here at Home-Free Haven would like to welcome you to our organization! We are a cold-season shelter that operates in the Shoals area. As of this season, we do not have a building for our own intake center. However, our intake location can be found in front of the Walgreens on 109 E Dr Hicks Blvd, Florence, AL 35630, the same location Room in the Inn would previously operate. We are operating in a similar fashion as RITI, where each night we will use a different facility to provide room and board for our guests. We will be open on Sunday, Tuesday, Thursday, and Friday (as well as emergency nights as needed). We look forward to having you as a guest!

Rules

In order to stay with Homefree Haven, you must agree to the following rules. If these rules are broken, we can not allow you to stay with us:

  • I agree to follow the 5 Simple Rules while staying with Homefree Haven.
  • I agree to a bag and clothing search when signing in each evening. Any weapons or illegal substances will be confiscated.
  • I agree to surrender any weapons I may possess while I am staying with Homefree Haven. Any weapons turned in will be returned when you leave.
  • I understand that HH does not and cannot provide prescription assistance, transportation to and from work, or financial loans.
  • I agree to follow the policy by not asking for additional needs or favors from any volunteer or church host group.
  • I understand that if I leave the host church after checking in, I will not be able to stay at Homefree Haven for the night.
  • I understand that if I do not follow the rules or policies of Homefree Haven, that I will be asked to leave and may not be allowed to stay with Homefree Haven for the rest of the season.
