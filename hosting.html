Hosting Info

Our organization could not provide for our guests without the help of the community. If you are an organization that is willing to provide room and board for even one night throughout the season of the 27th of November to the 17th of March, it would be greatly appreciated.

Hosting meetings will be held on November 5th and November 12th at 1:00 PM to 3:00 PM at Cross Point Church of Christ 1350 Cox Creek Pkwy, Florence, AL 35633. If you wish to sign up please ensure a member of your congregation is able to attend as a representative.

Our organization could not provide for our guests without the help of the community. If you are an organization that is willing to provide room and board for even one night throughout the season of the 27th of November to the 17th of March, it would be greatly appreciated.

What we provide as an organization:

  • Volunteers to assist before the guests arrive.
  • Security at intake where guests will be searched and checked for any criminal warrants.
  • Transportation for the guests from the intake location to the host church as well as back in the morning.
  • Employees to work the night and ensure security and satisfaction to our guests
  • Blankets, cots, food, toiletries, and basic clothing items.
  • Clean up the next morning as well as breakfast.

Your church's contributions are deeply valued. Your support is crucial to our mission. Any form of help, from hosting to volunteering, significantly aids our work at Homefree Haven. We welcome your involvement with our guests and thank you profoundly for your partnership in our ongoing success.

If you are interested in hosting with us, please call us at (256) 415-7278 or email us at contact@homefreehaven.org.
